Important requirements copied from the official page:

- General papers are poster presentations.
- General-paper deadline is 2026-06-22.
- Manuscript language: Japanese or English.
- Page limit: 4 pages or fewer, excluding references.
- Japanese abstract: 200 characters or fewer.
- Author names and affiliations must be included.
- Optional supplementary material is allowed.
- The 2026 template should be used because it changed from the previous year.
